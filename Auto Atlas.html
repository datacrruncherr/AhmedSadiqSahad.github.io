Project Description

The aim of this project is to combine all the needs and wants of an everyday person with regard to their vehicle. The project combines personal needs such as vehicle reminders such as service reminders, part changes, oil changes and even tracks the mileage and vehicle health of the vehicle along with broader requirements such as getting parts and services from a multitude of shops that can register with the website. .

Project Description: An application that Effectively Streamlines Vehicle Maintainance

TAuto Atlas is a comprehensive web and Android application designed to simplify the process of managing shops, vehicles, parts, and services related to the automotive industry. This project aims to provide a user-friendly interface for users to efficiently navigate through various features and perform necessary actions.

Key Features:

  1. Shop Management: Auto Atlas allows users to view a list of shops available in their area. Users can explore detailed information about each shop, including contact details, operating hours, and services offered. They can also add new shops or modify existing shop information as necessary.
  2. Vehicle Management: Users can manage their vehicles within the Auto Atlas system. They can add new vehicles, track vehicle details such as make, model, year, and mileage, and schedule routine maintenance or service appointments.
  3. Part Booking: To streamline the process of purchasing parts, Auto Atlas offers a convenient part booking feature. Users can select the desired parts, specify quantities, and add them to their shopping cart. They can then proceed to book the parts for pickup or delivery, providing necessary details such as preferred shop location or shipping address.
  4. Service Management: Auto Atlas enables users to schedule service appointments for their vehicles. They can browse through a list of available services offered by the shops in the network, such as oil changes, tire rotations, or brake inspections. Users can select the desired service, choose a convenient date and time, and book the appointment.

Auto Atlas is a powerful and user-friendly application that aims to revolutionize the way users manage their automotive needs. By providing a seamless interface for both web and Android platforms, Auto Atlas ensures that users can access its features anytime, anywhere. Whether it's finding the right shop, keeping track of vehicle information, browsing and booking parts, or scheduling service appointments, Auto Atlas simplifies the entire process, saving users valuable time and effort. With its intuitive design and comprehensive features, Auto Atlas is set to enhance the overall automotive experience, empowering users to efficiently manage their vehicles and connect with trusted shops and services. Embrace the future of automotive management with Auto Atlas.
